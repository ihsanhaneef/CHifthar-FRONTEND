About Us

This is a humble initiative taken up by the CH Mohammad Koya Chair for Studies on Developing Societies with the help of the msf team, student volunteers and a group of kind sponsors. Providing suhoor (the pre-dawn meal) and iftar for over 400 students, the CH chair iftar has become a big blessing for the fasting students.
